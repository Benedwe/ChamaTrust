# ChamaTrust Investor Demo

## One-Liner

ChamaTrust is M-Pesa-simple community banking with Avalanche-grade transparency for African savings groups.

## Problem

Millions of Chama, Vicoba, SACCO, and informal groups coordinate savings and loans through chat, paper ledgers, and trust. Members want Mobile Money simplicity, but groups need better transparency, governance, fraud controls, and access to capital.

## Solution

ChamaTrust lets members deposit with Mobile Money, vote on loans, build trust scores, and track group finances while blockchain records treasury activity invisibly in the background.

## Demo Flow

1. Open the dashboard and show treasury balance, savings growth, health score, rankings, and loan performance.
2. Select M-Pesa and trigger the animated deposit flow.
3. Open the loan proposal card for Amina's school fees request.
4. Vote and show the quorum progress.
5. Show the transaction explorer with provider and Avalanche activity.
6. Highlight ChamaTrust Advisor recommendations and trust badges.

## Why Now

- Mobile Money is trusted and widely adopted across East Africa.
- Community savings groups already have strong offline behavior.
- Avalanche offers fast settlement, low fees, and Subnet scalability.
- Stablecoins and regulated tokenized deposits can modernize community finance without forcing users to learn crypto.

## Business Model

- Transaction fee on deposits and withdrawals.
- SaaS subscription for SACCO dashboards.
- Loan origination fee for partner lenders.
- Analytics and risk tooling for financial institutions.
- White-label community finance infrastructure.

## Expansion

- Agricultural financing.
