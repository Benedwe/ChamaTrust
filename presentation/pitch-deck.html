Investor Pitch

ChamaTrust

M-Pesa-simple community banking with transparent treasury controls for African savings groups.

Pilot partners, regulated fintech/Mobile Money partners, and seed funding to move from demo to live pilots.

Problem

Community finance works, but the tools are fragile.

Fragmented records

Contributions are often tracked through chat, spreadsheets, paper ledgers, or one treasurer's phone.

Limited visibility

Members lack real-time clarity on balances, loan approvals, repayments, and withdrawals.

Trust does not scale

Fraud, late repayments, informal governance, and missing records can damage strong social groups.

Why Now

Mobile Money has reached infrastructure scale.

2.3Bregistered mobile money accounts in 2025
$2.1Tannual mobile money transaction value
84%adults in LMICs who own a mobile phone

ChamaTrust uses blockchain as the trust layer underneath familiar payment flows.

Solution

A mobile-first operating system for savings groups.

  • Members join, deposit, vote, borrow, repay, and withdraw through familiar flows.
  • Group leaders get cleaner ledgers, stronger governance, fraud flags, and member reputation.
  • Partners get auditable, consent-based group finance records for responsible services.

Simple user actions, serious infrastructure underneath.

Business Model

Multiple revenue paths from one trusted record.

Transaction fees

Deposits, withdrawals, and loan disbursements.

SaaS

Admin dashboards for SACCOs, NGOs, cooperatives, and operators.

Partner revenue

Loan origination, risk analytics, and white-label infrastructure.

Go To Market

Start with high-trust pilots, then expand through institutions.

Launch users

Chama, Vicoba, and SACCO groups with active Mobile Money behavior.

Channels

SACCO networks, NGOs, religious communities, women-led savings groups, and local agents.

Partners

Mobile Money providers, KYC providers, regulated settlement rails, and lenders.

Success metrics

Repeat contributions, active votes, reconciled payments, repayment visibility, and retained groups.

Moat

The operating record becomes the advantage.

  • Community-specific UX built around real savings group behavior.
  • Transparent treasury history without exposing members to blockchain complexity.
  • Reputation from contributions, voting, repayment, and participation.
  • Provider reconciliation across Mobile Money and on-chain records.

Roadmap

Validate, pilot, then harden for production.

Phase 1

Demo dashboard, Mobile Money adapters, Fuji contracts, voting, and advisor insights.

Phase 2

M-Pesa sandbox, webhooks, admin portal, KYC, multisig, SMS and WhatsApp reminders.

Phase 3

Regulated settlement support, compliance reporting, fraud analytics, AI scoring, USSD, and Subnet readiness.

Funding Use

Seed capital should reduce execution risk.

  • Mobile Money sandbox-to-production integrations.
  • Security review for API, custody assumptions, and smart contracts.
  • Pilot onboarding, training, support, and field operations.
  • Compliance, admin tools, reconciliation, USSD, WhatsApp, and analytics.
